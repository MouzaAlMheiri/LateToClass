Short Film Trailer

Late to Class was created by three emirati NYUAD students, following the story of a friend who has a series of

predicaments while trying to get to her class. While Meera is trying to get to her class her friends Mouza and

Mahra are there wondering where she might have gone oblivious to the challenges shes facing.
