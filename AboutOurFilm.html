About Our Film

Late to Class follows the chaotic morning of three friends as they rush through school hallways, racing against the clock to make it on time.

Meera takes the lead as the main character, determined yet constantly stumbling into obstacles.

Mouza and Mahra support her with quick humor and unexpected teamwork, keeping the journey lively and unpredictable.

This short film captures the comedy of everyday school life, showing how friendship and laughter can turn even the most stressful mornings into a memorable adventure.
